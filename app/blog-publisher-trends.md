# 8 Top Publisher Topics for Blog Posts

Based on AdExchanger & Digiday research:

## 1. First-Party Data: The New Currency for Publisher Revenue
- Focus: How publishers can monetize their first-party data
- Angle: AI training, audience licensing, targeting precision
- Revenue opportunity: $50K-$500K+ from data licensing
- Sources: AdExchanger "AI Has Already Decided", Future AI training

## 2. Publisher Independence Strategy: Breaking Free from Ad Tech Middlemen
- Focus: Direct sales, reducing intermediaries
- Angle: DTC advertising, owned audience monetization
- Revenue opportunity: 30-50% margin improvement
- Sources: AdExchanger "Ruthless Independence"

## 3. Video Advertising: Why Publishers Must Invest in 2026
- Focus: CTV, outstream, sponsored video
- Angle: Video CPMs are 3-5x higher than display
- Revenue opportunity: $100K-$1M depending on format
- Sources: Digiday research on video growth drivers

## 4. AI & Agentic Advertising: The Future of Ad Optimization
- Focus: How AI agents optimize bids, pricing, targeting
- Angle: Real-time decisioning vs. traditional auctions
- Revenue opportunity: 15-25% revenue lift from optimization
- Sources: AdExchanger "Agentic Era"

## 5. Programmatic Diversification: Beyond Google AdX
- Focus: Alternative SSPs, header bidding, private exchanges
- Angle: Don't rely on one demand source
- Revenue opportunity: 20-50% lift from demand diversification
- Sources: Digiday survey on programmatic focus

## 6. AI Content Licensing: Turning Your Content into Revenue
- Focus: Licensed content to AI platforms (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, etc.)
- Angle: News Corp, NYT approach to AI licensing
- Revenue opportunity: $100K-$5M depending on traffic
- Sources: Digiday "AI Licensing Opportunities"

## 7. Publisher Stability Strategy: From Growth to Profitability
- Focus: Cost optimization, inventory quality over quantity
- Angle: Sustainable business model
- Revenue opportunity: 20-30% cost reduction
- Sources: AdExchanger "Publishers Don't Need a Reset"

## 8. Performance Advertising: Proving ROI to Brand Advertisers
- Focus: Conversions, LTV, customer acquisition metrics
- Angle: Moving from impressions to outcomes
- Revenue opportunity: Premium pricing for measurable results
- Sources: AdExchanger CTV roundup, advertiser demands
